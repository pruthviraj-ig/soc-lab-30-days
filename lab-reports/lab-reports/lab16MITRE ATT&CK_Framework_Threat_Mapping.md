## What is MITRE ATT&CK

MITRE ATT&CK is a globally recognised knowledge base that categorises adversary behaviour into:

- Tactics (the attacker’s objective)
- Techniques (how the objective is achieved)
- Sub-techniques (specific implementations)

It provides structured mapping for real-world cyber attacks.
