## Key Concepts

- Humans are the weakest link in cybersecurity
- Attackers often target people instead of systems
- This is called **Social Engineering**

## Common Human-Based Attacks

- Phishing emails
- Deepfake voice/video scams
- Impersonation (CEO / IT support)
- Fake job offers
- USB drop attacks
- Insider threats

## Why Humans Are Targeted

- Easier than breaking secure systems
- Can reveal credentials and sensitive data
- Trust, fear, urgency and curiosity are exploited

## Attacker Techniques

- Create urgency ("Act now")
- Pretend to be trusted authority
- Use emotional triggers
- Fake login pages
- Malicious links

## SOC Defense Measures

### Mitigation
- Anti-phishing tools
- Antivirus / EDR
- Email filtering
- Security awareness training
- Trust-but-verify principle

### Detection
- SOC team investigates remaining attacks
- Monitor alerts and suspicious activity
- User reports are important
